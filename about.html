About EPSchedule

Schedules, the EPS way

View your schedule anywhere - on your laptop, tablet, or phone.

Your schedule is always accurate - even when Assembly is after H period, or exams are the next day.

All your info in one place

See who's in your History class, or find out when you can meet with your Chemistry teacher.

Integrated campus maps let you find the best way to get to your Lit room.

For students, by students

EPSchedule is an open source project, led by Gavin Uberti.

If you're curious about exactly how something works, you can check out EPSchedule on GitHub.

Goals

EPSchedule was designed to:

  • Help students visualize their schedules
  • Bring together separate sources of information
  • Allow students to easily meet with friends and teachers

Data Security

EPSchedule uses industry-standard best practices, including:

  • Mandatory HTTPS encryption
  • Bcrypt password hashing/salting
  • Eastsideprep.org email address verification

Privacy Information

EPSchedule collects statistical data in order to help users receive the best experience. This data will never be shared with third parties.
