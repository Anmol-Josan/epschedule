About EPSchedule

EPSchedule is an open-source schedule app designed by Gavin Uberti

Secure

EPSchedule is built on state-of-the-art security. All traffic is sent of HTTPS and TLS, and passwords are hashed via Bcrypt.

Powerful

EPSchedule allows for easy viewing of things such as which rooms are free during your study hall, or how to get to your chemistry class.

Open

EPSchedule is fully open source. If you're curious about exactly how something works, view EPSchedule on Github.

Learn about your schedule beforehand

Don't wait to learn who's in your class until the first day. Or see if it's possible for you to take History with your favorite teacher. Or find which rooms are open to practice your Spanish presentation in.

Day-by-day looks at what's coming

See the day before whether Assembly is before or after H period. Or learn exactly what time your Lit final starts. Or discover what your schedule will be like on Field Day
